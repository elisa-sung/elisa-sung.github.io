Art is something that stimulates an individual's thoughts, emotions, beliefs, or ideas through the senses. Works of art can be explicitly made for this purpose or interpreted on the basis of images or objects. However, even fine art often has goals beyond pure creativity and self-expression.

Why is art important

Art is important because it encompasses all the developmental domains in child development. Art lends itself to physical development and the enhancement of fine and gross motor skills. Children learn about themselves and others through art activities. It really helps them build self-esteem. Also, art, in any form, can give people emotions that can lift up their spirit and make them more driven than ever. ... And this is the mere reason why art is important in our daily lives.
